About Us

We accept all homeless, injured, orphaned, unwanted, lost, abandoned and mistreated dogs. Every dog brought to our door is welcome, regardless of age, breed, temperament or medical condition. We provide care, medical treatment and behavioral assessment before finding them the loving homes and second chance they all deserve.
